Welcome to the Astronomy Club of Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kottayam! Our club is dedicated to promoting the study and appreciation of astronomy among students at IIITK.

Through our club, students can explore the wonders of the universe and learn about the latest discoveries in astronomy. Our members have access to telescopes, observatories, and other equipment that allow them to observe celestial bodies up close.

We host a variety of events and activities throughout the year, including stargazing parties, lectures from guest speakers, and astronomy-related workshops. These events provide a great opportunity for members to meet other students who share their passion for astronomy and to learn from experts in the field.

Our club is open to students of all backgrounds and experience levels. Whether you're a seasoned stargazer or a beginner who's just starting to explore the universe, we welcome you to join us and discover the wonders of astronomy.

If you're interested in joining our club, or if you have any questions about our events or activities, please don't hesitate to contact us. We look forward to exploring the universe with you!
